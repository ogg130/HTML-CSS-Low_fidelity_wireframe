Things to do

Amazing attactions to visit during your stay...

You have experienced much in life and deserve relaxation!



Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the romantic beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano.

However, there are other things to do, including visiting a local history museam, going on chartered fishing

tours, snorkeling, zip-lining in the rainforest, vising several pubs, including a microbrewery, dancing at new

dance club, seeing a movie, taking helicopter rides, playing at an arcade, visiting art galleries and bowling.

Also, a nine hole golf course should be operational by next year. Many of these activities are located in

Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.



Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white,

sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Other populate activites include boat or bus tours of the island,

hikes in the rainforest, or visits to Taniti's active volcano.
