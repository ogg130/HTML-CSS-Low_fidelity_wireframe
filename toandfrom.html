To and from the island

We dont want you to leave, but if you must...

Almost all visitors arrive to FictitiousIsland by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf

Bay for one night per week. FictitiousIsland is served by a small airport that can accomodate small jets and

propeller planes. FictitiousIsland is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the

island within the next few years.
